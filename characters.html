Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates

Age: 19
Height: 174 cm
Birthday: May 5
Devil Fruit: Gum-Gum Fruit

Current Bounty

3,000,000,000 Berries

About

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain and main protagonist of One Piece. He ate the Gum-Gum Fruit, giving him rubber powers. Despite his carefree nature, Luffy's unwavering determination and loyalty to his crew have made him one of the most feared pirates in the world.

Dream

To become the Pirate King and find the legendary treasure, the One Piece.

Abilities

Rubber body manipulation, Haki mastery, Gear transformations (Gear 2, Gear 3, Gear 4, Gear 5), conqueror's haki.

Roronoa Zoro

Swordsman & Vice Captain

Age: 21
Height: 181 cm
Birthday: November 11
Sword Style: Three Sword Style

Current Bounty

1,111,000,000 Berries

About

Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the crew and the second strongest member after Luffy. Known for his green hair, three swords, and serious demeanor. He is fiercely loyal to Luffy and serves as the de facto vice-captain.

Dream

To become the world's greatest swordsman.

Abilities

Masterful swordsmanship, three-sword technique, Haki mastery, sheer strength and endurance.

Nami

Navigator & Cartographer

Age: 20
Height: 170 cm
Birthday: July 3
Weapon: Climate Baton

Current Bounty

366,000,000 Berries

About

Nami is the navigator and the second recruited member of the Straw Hat Pirates. Despite her initial reluctance, she has become deeply devoted to the crew. She is intelligent, skilled in navigation and combat, and serves as the crew's financial advisor.

Dream

To create a complete map of the world.

Abilities

Expert navigation, cartography, weather science, combat with climate weapons.

Usopp

Sniper & Storyteller

Age: 19
Height: 176 cm
Birthday: April 1
Weapon: Slingshot & Gadgets

Current Bounty

300,000,000 Berries

About

Usopp is the sniper and sharpshooter of the Straw Hat Pirates. Known for his tall tales and creative abilities, he is the son of the famous pirate Yasopp. Despite his cowardly nature, he has grown into a reliable crew member with incredible accuracy.

Dream

To become a great warrior of the sea and earn the title of a mighty pirate.

Abilities

Exceptionally accurate shooting, creative weapon development, courage in critical moments.

Sanji

Cook & Fighter

Age: 21
Height: 180 cm
Birthday: March 2
Fighting Style: Leg-based Martial Arts

Current Bounty

1,032,000,000 Berries

About

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the strongest members. He fights exclusively with his legs and is known for his womanizing nature and chivalry. Despite his carefree attitude, he is a skilled fighter and deeply cares for the crew.

Dream

To find the legendary All Blue, a sea where all fish from every ocean gather.

Abilities

Powerful leg-based combat techniques, Haki mastery, superhuman strength and speed.

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor & Reindeer

Age: 17
Height: 88 cm (188 cm as human)
Birthday: December 24
Devil Fruit: Human-Human Fruit

Current Bounty

1,000,000,000 Berries

About

Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. Originally a reindeer, he ate the Human-Human Fruit and gained human intelligence. Despite his cute appearance, he is a skilled doctor and powerful fighter with multiple transformation abilities.

Dream

To become a great doctor and find a cure for all diseases.

Abilities

Medical expertise, multiple human transformations, hybrid forms, combat training.

Nico Robin

Archaeologist & Scholar

Age: 30
Height: 188 cm
Birthday: February 6
Devil Fruit: Flower-Flower Fruit

Current Bounty

930,000,000 Berries

About

Nico Robin is the archaeologist and one of the oldest members of the crew. She has the Flower-Flower Fruit powers allowing her to sprout multiple body parts. Once a member of the Baroque Works, she joined the Straw Hats to find the truth of the Void Century.

Dream

To uncover the true history of the world and understand the Void Century.

Abilities

Ancient history knowledge, archaeology expertise, Flower-Flower Fruit powers, combat experience.

Franky

Shipwright & Cyborg

Age: 36
Height: 225 cm
Birthday: March 9
Enhancements: Cyborg Body

Current Bounty

394,000,000 Berries

About

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates and captain of the Thousand Sunny. He is a cyborg with incredible strength and weapons built into his body. Despite his tough appearance, he has a soft heart and loves the crew deeply.

Dream

To build a ship worthy of the Pirate King and sail the world.

Abilities

Shipbuilding expertise, cyborg weapons and enhancements, superhuman strength.

Brook

Musician & Swordsman

Age: 90 (appears 38)
Height: 277 cm
Birthday: April 3
Devil Fruit: Soul-Soul Fruit

Current Bounty

383,000,000 Berries

About

Brook is the musician and swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a living skeleton thanks to the Soul-Soul Fruit, which allowed his soul to return to his body. He is the oldest crew member by age despite appearing much younger.

Dream

To reunite with his old friend Laboon and play music across the world.

Abilities

Master swordsman, musical talents, Soul-Soul Fruit powers, soul-based attacks.
