Devil Fruits Encyclopedia

Explore the mysterious powers that shape the world of One Piece

Paramecia Type

Paramecia Devil Fruits grant their users superhuman abilities that don't fall into the elemental or animal transformation categories. These fruits provide a wide variety of powers, from body manipulation to creating substances.

Gomu Gomu no Mi

Paramecia

User: Monkey D. Luffy

The Rubber-Rubber Fruit grants the user the properties of rubber, allowing them to stretch, bounce, and manipulate their body in extraordinary ways. This fruit turned Luffy into the rubber man capable of incredible feats.

Notable Abilities

  • Gomu Gomu no Pistol: Basic stretching punch attack
  • Gomu Gomu no Bazooka: Double-handed stretching punch
  • Gomu Gomu no Gatling: Rapid-fire punch barrage
  • Gomu Gomu no Rocket: Full-body stretching propulsion
  • Gear Techniques: Advanced power-ups (Second, Third, Fourth, Fifth Gear)

Hana Hana no Mi

Paramecia

User: Nico Robin

The Flower-Flower Fruit allows the user to sprout multiple body parts (limbs, eyes, mouths) from any surface. This ability gives Robin unparalleled versatility in combat and information gathering.

Notable Abilities

  • Fleur: Sprout multiple limbs for attacks
  • Clutch: Grab and restrain opponents
  • Mil Fleur: Thousand Flower attack
  • Cien Fleur: Hundred Flower defense
  • Espionage Capabilities: Gather intelligence from multiple locations

Yami Yami no Mi

Paramecia

User: Marshall D. Teach (Blackbeard)

The Darkness-Darkness Fruit allows the user to create and control darkness. This fruit gives Blackbeard the ability to nullify other Devil Fruit powers and manipulate gravitational forces.

Notable Abilities

  • Devil Fruit Nullification: Cancel other Devil Fruit abilities
  • Black Hole: Create crushing gravitational fields
  • Darkness Manipulation: Control and shape darkness
  • Quake Integration: Combine with Gura Gura no Mi

Suke Suke no Mi

Paramecia

User: Absalom

The Clear-Clear Fruit grants the ability to become invisible and make objects invisible by touch. This fruit provides perfect stealth capabilities.

Notable Abilities

  • Invisibility: Become completely invisible
  • Object Invisibility: Make touched objects invisible
  • Stealth Operations: Perfect for ambushes and espionage

Logia Type

Logia Devil Fruits grant their users the ability to create, control, and transform their body into natural elements. These are considered the most powerful type of Devil Fruit, giving users elemental control and intangibility.

Mera Mera no Mi

Logia

User: Portgas D. Ace (Former), Sabo

The Flame-Flame Fruit allows the user to create, control, and transform into fire. This Logia fruit gives the user complete mastery over flames and immunity to most fire-based attacks.

Notable Abilities

  • Fire Manipulation: Create and control flames
  • Fire Transformation: Become living fire
  • Fire Immunity: Immune to fire attacks
  • Hiken: Fire Fist technique
  • Entei: Fire Cloud technique

Suna Suna no Mi

Logia

User: Crocodile

The Sand-Sand Fruit allows the user to create, control, and transform into sand. This fruit gives Crocodile desert-manipulating powers and the ability to dehydrate opponents.

Notable Abilities

  • Sand Manipulation: Control sand and create sandstorms
  • Desert Spada: Sand blade attacks
  • Dehydration: Absorb moisture from targets
  • Sables: Sand shield defense

Pika Pika no Mi

Logia

User: Borsalino (Kizaru)

The Glint-Glint Fruit allows the user to create, control, and transform into light. This fruit gives Admiral Kizaru incredible speed and light-based attacks.

Notable Abilities

  • Light Speed: Move at the speed of light
  • Laser Attacks: Yasakani no Magatama
  • Light Manipulation: Create light constructs
  • Intangibility: Become light to avoid attacks

Zoan Type

Zoan Devil Fruits allow users to transform into animals and hybrid forms between human and animal. These fruits are divided into Normal, Ancient, and Mythical Zoan types, with Mythical being the rarest and most powerful.

Hito Hito no Mi

Zoan

User: Tony Tony Chopper

The Human-Human Fruit allows the user to transform into a human and hybrid forms. This fruit gave Chopper human intelligence and the ability to transform into various forms for different situations.

Notable Abilities

  • Human Transformation: Become fully human
  • Hybrid Forms: Various human-reindeer hybrids
  • Rumble Ball Enhancement: Temporary power boosts
  • Monster Point: Ultimate transformation

Ushi Ushi no Mi, Model: Giraffe

Zoan

User: Dalton

The Cow-Cow Fruit, Model: Giraffe allows the user to transform into a giraffe and hybrid forms. This fruit gives Dalton giraffe-like abilities including long neck attacks.

Notable Abilities

  • Giraffe Transformation: Become a giraffe
  • Elongated Neck: Extend neck for attacks
  • Hybrid Strength: Enhanced physical power

Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix

Zoan (Mythical)

User: Marco

The Bird-Bird Fruit, Model: Phoenix is a Mythical Zoan that allows the user to transform into a phoenix and hybrid forms. This legendary fruit grants Marco regenerative abilities and fire manipulation.

Notable Abilities

  • Phoenix Transformation: Become a mythical phoenix
  • Regeneration: Heal from severe injuries
  • Blue Flames: Phoenix fire manipulation
  • Immortality: Cannot die from normal means

Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Wolf

Zoan

User: Jabra

The Dog-Dog Fruit, Model: Wolf allows the user to transform into a wolf and hybrid forms. This fruit enhances Jabra's physical abilities and gives him wolf-like senses.

Notable Abilities

  • Wolf Transformation: Become a wolf
  • Enhanced Senses: Superior smell and hearing
  • Hybrid Strength: Increased physical power
