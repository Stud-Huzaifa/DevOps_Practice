About

Brook is the musician and swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, and the only member who is technically undead. He ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi (Revive-Revive Fruit), which brought him back to life after death. His dream is to reunite with his old friend Laboon, the whale he left behind 50 years ago.

Originally from the Rumbar Pirates, Brook was the only survivor of his crew after a battle with a Sea King. He spent decades as a skeleton before meeting the Straw Hats. His cheerful personality and musical talents make him a beloved member of the crew.

Abilities & Techniques

  • Yomi Yomi no Mi
    A Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows Brook to return from death once, making him immortal.
  • Soul Solid
    Brook can separate his soul from his body, allowing him to phase through objects and become intangible.
  • Fencing Skills
    Master swordsman with incredible speed and precision, able to cut through steel and stone.
  • Ice Sword Techniques
    Special techniques that create ice effects with his sword swings.
  • Musical Abilities
    Exceptional violin and piano skills, able to play soul-stirring music that can affect emotions.
  • French Cuisine
    Brook's cooking style that creates dishes with bone motifs and surprising flavors.
