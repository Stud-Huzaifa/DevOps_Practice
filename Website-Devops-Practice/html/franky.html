About

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates and a cyborg with superhuman strength. Originally known as Cutty Flam, he was a talented shipwright who rebuilt himself after a near-fatal accident. His dream is to build and sail on the most powerful ship in the world.

Franky was once a member of the Franky Family, a group of dismantlers in Water 7. After the crew helped him, he rebuilt their ship, the Thousand Sunny, and joined them as a crew member. His inventions and mechanical expertise are invaluable to the Straw Hats.

Abilities & Techniques

  • Cyborg Modifications
    Franky's body is heavily modified with mechanical enhancements, giving him superhuman strength and durability.
  • Strong Right
    Franky's most powerful attack, a devastating punch that can destroy buildings.
  • Coup de Vent
    A powerful kick that creates a shockwave capable of blowing away enemies.
  • Weapons Left
    Franky's left arm contains various weapons and tools for combat and utility.
  • Shipbuilding Expertise
    Master shipwright capable of building and repairing ships with incredible precision and innovation.
  • Invention Creation
    Creates various gadgets and weapons, including cola-powered devices and mechanical enhancements.

Dressrosa Arc
