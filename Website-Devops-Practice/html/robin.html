About

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and the only survivor of the destroyed island of Ohara. Known as the "Devil Child" due to her Devil Fruit abilities, Robin was once hunted by the World Government. Her dream is to find the Rio Poneglyph, which contains the true history of the world.

Despite her calm and composed demeanor, Robin has a deep understanding of history and ancient civilizations. She joined the Straw Hats after they risked their lives to save her from execution, and she has become a crucial member of the crew with her vast knowledge and abilities.

Abilities & Techniques

  • Hana Hana no Mi
    A Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows Robin to sprout multiple body parts from any surface.
  • Fleur
    Basic technique that creates multiple arms from surfaces to perform various actions.
  • Gigantesco Mano
    Creates giant hands that can crush enemies or manipulate large objects.
  • Cien Fleur
    Creates a hundred arms for devastating attacks and defense.
  • Archaeological Knowledge
    Extensive knowledge of ancient civilizations, Poneglyphs, and the true history of the world.
  • Clutch
    Creates hands that can grab and restrain multiple enemies simultaneously.
