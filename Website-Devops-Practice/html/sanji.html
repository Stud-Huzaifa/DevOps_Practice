About

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and a master martial artist specializing in leg-based combat. Known for his gentlemanly behavior towards women and his chivalrous nature, Sanji is also infamous for his perverted tendencies. His dream is to find the legendary sea known as "All Blue," where all the fish from every sea come together.

As a former prince of the Germa Kingdom, Sanji rejected his royal heritage to pursue his passion for cooking. His incredible speed and powerful kicks make him one of the strongest fighters in the crew.

Abilities & Techniques

  • Black Leg Style
    Sanji's signature fighting style focusing on powerful kicks and leg-based attacks.
  • Diable Jambe
    A technique where Sanji heats his leg to extreme temperatures using friction, creating powerful flaming kicks.
  • Sky Walk
    Sanji's ability to run through the air by kicking off invisible platforms.
  • Culinary Expertise
    Master chef skills with exceptional cooking abilities and knowledge of various cuisines.
  • Enhanced Speed
    Incredible speed and reflexes, allowing him to move faster than the eye can see.
  • Haki Proficiency
    Mastery of Armament Haki and Observation Haki, enhancing his combat abilities significantly.
